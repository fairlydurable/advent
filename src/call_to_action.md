# Learn more

**WORK IN PROGRESS**

There's so much more to learn and explore in Mojo. Here are links to
explore what's next:

- 🔥 In **Mojo Quest**, you'll learn Mojo by solving code problems right in
  the browser. [Get started](https://quest.mojolang.org)

- 🔥 Check out our **Mojo cheat sheets**. Each preview matches your light or
  dark theme. Click a preview to open it full size, or download a PNG or PDF
  to keep.
  [Take a look](https://mojolang.org/nightly/docs/reference/cheat-sheets/)

- 🔥 Explore our **standard library API reference**.
  [Get going](https://mojolang.org/nightly/docs/std/)

- 🔥 Visit our **Mojo language online reference**. It's a concise reference for
  the Mojo language syntax, keywords, and more.
  [Find your information](https://mojolang.org/nightly/docs/reference/)

- 🔥 Follow our **Get Started with Mojo** tutorial.
  [Learn more](https://mojolang.org/nightly/docs/manual/get-started/)

- 🔥 Visit our **Mojo language manual**.
  [Resource for learning](https://mojolang.org/nightly/docs/manual/)
